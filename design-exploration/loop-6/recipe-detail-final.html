Thai Basil Chicken

★★★★★

A quick, punchy stir-fry with holy basil, bird's eye chili, and crispy garlic. The kind of weeknight meal that tastes like it took an hour but didn't.

Prep: 15 min
Cook: 10 min
Total: 25 min
4 servings
Easy
ThaiChicken QuickStir-Fry Spicy
View original recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 Thai bird's eye chilis, sliced
  • 2 cups Thai holy basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

1
Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat until smoking. Add garlic and chilis, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant but not burned.
2
Add ground chicken, breaking it apart with a spatula. Cook for 3-4 minutes until no longer pink, pressing it against the wok to get some crispy bits.
3
Add oyster sauce, fish sauce, soy sauce, and sugar. Toss everything together for another minute until the chicken is well-coated and slightly caramelized.
4
Kill the heat. Throw in the basil leaves and fold them through the chicken — they'll wilt in seconds from residual heat. Serve immediately over jasmine rice with a fried egg on top.
